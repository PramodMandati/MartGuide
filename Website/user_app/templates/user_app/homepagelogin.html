This is the home page of the MartGuide desktop application. The owner has to login with the given credentials.

After login, the below page appears and the owner can calculate the total amount of the items taken by a customer. The below items has to be added by the owner itself.

In this page, the owner can add the items with their respective prices.
NOTE: The items will be available in different quantities with respective prices, so the owner should be carefull while adding the items. The owner can mention the items like Milk_1Ltr.

In this page, the owner can remove the items which are added by him/her.

This is the main page of the entire application. Here the owner knows the items which are taking frequently with the other items. Here the owner has to mention the two values. The first one is Confidence i.e., this is the percentage chance of taking a item(X) with the other item(Y). And the other one is Support i.e., this is the percentage of taking both X and Y in the entire transaction history.

NOTE: An important thing in the application is, the items added and remove in the respective pages are not reflected immediately. The changes are modified after the owner is logout from the application and login again.
